VEG SANDWICH

Preparation Time

10 minutes

Difficulty

Easy

Ingredients

  • 4 bread slices
  • Butter
  • 1 cucumber (sliced)
  • 1 tomato (sliced)
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Spread butter evenly on bread slices.
  2. Layer cucumber and tomato slices.
  3. Sprinkle salt and pepper.
  4. Cover with another slice.
  5. Cut and serve fresh.
