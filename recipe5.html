White Sauce Pasta

Preparation Time

30 minutes

Difficulty

Hard

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil pasta.
  2. Prepare white sauce using butter, flour, milk.
  3. Add cheese and seasoning.
  4. Mix pasta with sauce.
  5. Serve hot.
