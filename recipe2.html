Chocolate Mug Cake

Preparation Time

5 minutes

Difficulty

Easy - Medium

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp flour
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 2 tbsp oil

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a mug.
  2. Stir until smooth batter forms.
  3. Microwave for 2 minutes.
  4. Let it cool slightly.
  5. Enjoy warm.
