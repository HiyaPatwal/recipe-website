Banana Bread

Preparation Time

1 hour

Difficulty

Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mash bananas and mix all ingredients.
  3. Pour batter into a baking tin.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes.
  5. Cool before serving.
