Paneer Butter Masala

Preparation Time

40 minutes

Difficulty

Medium - Hard

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • Butter
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Prepare tomato-onion gravy.
  2. Add spices and cook well.
  3. Add paneer cubes.
  4. Cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with naan.
